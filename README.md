Reecon satellites can orbit at different heights depending on their intended use. We 
would probably want to do a fun mechanic where you can "shift" between heights, going 
from GeoStationary Earth Orbit (~35,800k), down to middle earth or low earth orbit.

We could have three "satellites" at different levels that you hop between. Different 
resolutions and capabilities at each level.
